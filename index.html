How to Play

Select a mode, then take turns placing marks. Align symbols to win!


2-player Rules:


1.Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in an empty square.
2.The first to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
3.If all 9 cells are filled and no one wins, the game is a draw.

3-player Rules:


1.Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O or △) in an empty square.
2.The first to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
3.If all 9 cells are filled and no one wins, the game is a draw.

4-player Rules:


1.Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O or △ or ☐) in an empty square.
2.The first to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
3.If all 25 cells are filled and no one wins, the game is a draw.

About

This is a multi-player Tic Tac Toe game made for the web with animations, sounds, and fun logic!
